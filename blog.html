Bank ATM vs White Label ATM: Which is Better for Franchise?

November 2025 6 min read

If you're considering entering the ATM business, understanding the difference between Bank ATMs and White Label ATMs (WLA) is crucial. Here's everything you need to know to make an informed decision.

🏦 What is a White Label ATM (WLA)?

White Label ATMs are owned and operated by non-bank entities that are licensed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). Unlike bank ATMs, WLAs don't display any bank branding and can accept cards from all banks.

Our WLA Partners: India One, EPS Bancs, TSI Findi, and Vakrangee - all RBI-licensed operators providing reliable ATM services across India.

Why WLA ATMs Are the Future

  • Bank ATM Closures: 90% of ATM market opportunity comes from banks closing their ATMs
  • Low Penetration: Only 15 ATMs per 1 lakh people - huge growth potential
  • Franchise Model: Banks don't offer franchises - WLA is your only option
  • Average ROI of 22-30%: Significantly higher returns than traditional investments
