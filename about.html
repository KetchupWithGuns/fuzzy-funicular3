❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to the most common questions about starting your ATM franchise business.

What is the minimum investment required for an ATM franchise?

Investment ranges by location and ATM model, but most projects start between ₹5 lakh and ₹10 lakh including setup and approvals.

How much monthly income can I expect?

Monthly income depends on transaction volume and location quality. Many partners target recurring returns between ₹30,000 and ₹60,000.

How long does it take to get ROI?

Typical ROI is achieved in 12 to 18 months, depending on site performance, operational uptime, and transaction consistency.

What are the ongoing operational costs?

Recurring costs generally include site rent, power usage, cash handling, connectivity, and maintenance support.

Do I need technical knowledge to operate an ATM?

No advanced technical background is required. Partners receive onboarding support, operational guidance, and escalation channels.

How is cash management handled?

Cash loading and balancing are handled through approved cash management processes and partner bank or vendor workflows.

What locations work best for ATM placement?

High-footfall areas such as markets, transport hubs, institutions, and dense residential/commercial zones typically perform better.

Are there any hidden costs or fees?

No hidden charges are added. All expected costs are documented during evaluation and agreement stages.

What support do you provide after franchise setup?

Post-launch support covers technical assistance, performance reviews, compliance guidance, and ongoing partner coordination.

Can I operate multiple ATM franchises?

Yes. Many partners expand to multiple sites once first-location performance stabilizes and operations are standardized.
